Nick Loeloff About Me

Nick Loeloff is a third year Industrial Design major. He is proficient in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign, Rhino 7, Keyshot 9, and drawing and painting. He has had professional experience as a freelance artist for Mirror Sound LLC, completing 4 commisioned murals for Somerset County Golf Courses NJ, and also was a graphic design intern for Pro Sports Outlook in Falls Church VA.
